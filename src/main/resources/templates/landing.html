Food assistance (SNAP)

SNAP helps low income Minnesotans get the food they need. You’ll get an EBT card that you can use like a debit card to buy groceries at most stores and farmer’s markets.

Cash programs

Cash assistance helps people meet their basic needs until they are able to support themselves. You will get an EBT card that you can use like a debit card. Here are the most common cash programs:

  • Diversionary Work Program (DWP)
  • General Assistance
  • Group Residential Housing (GRH)
  • Mn Family Investment Program (MFIP)
  • Mn Supplemental Aid (MSA)
  • Refugee Cash Assitance (RCA)
